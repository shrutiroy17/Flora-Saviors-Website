One Valley. One Vision.
8,000 Trees. Endless Impact.

Flora Saviors is a non-profit organisation.We aim to preserve the beauty and green cover in india through our initiative to plant trees, increase awareness and restore the rich ecosystem of the city and eventually the state.Be it the melted glacier, global warming , erratic weather conditions ,polluted natural resources or continuous soil erosion, we plan to fight these man-made modern challanges with a conscious towards environmental restoration.

Let's bring back the green glory of Dehradun! In 2021, our 'Reviving Doon Valley' mission sowed seeds of hope — but in 2025, we're determined to let them bloom. This monsoon, we aim to plant 10,000 native trees across the valley — not just to plant, but to nurture them over the next 3 years with dedicated care and commitment. With your support, we can make this dream a living, breathing reality.
